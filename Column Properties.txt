The Track Properties

1) Genre - the genre of the track
3) Year - the release year of the recording.
4) Added - the earliest date you added the track to your collection.
5) Beats Per Minute (BPM) - The tempo of the song.
6) Energy - The energy of a song - the higher the value, the more energtic. song
7) Danceability - The higher the value, the easier it is to dance to this song.
8) Loudness (dB) - The higher the value, the louder the song.
9) Liveness - The higher the value, the more likely the song is a live recording.
10) Valence - The higher the value, the more positive mood for the song.
11) Length - The duration of the song.
12) Acousticness - The higher the value the more acoustic the song is.
13) Speechiness - The higher the value the more spoken word the song contains.
14) Popularity - The higher the value the more popular the song is.
15) Duration - The length of the song.
